Quick answers

Common questions before you write in.

How long does setup take?

Most people have their first room running in about twenty minutes. The app walks you through each device step by step — no electrician needed.

Will my devices work if the internet goes down?

Core automations — lights, locks, and climate — run on a hub in your home. Your system stays operational even during an outage.

Is there a monthly fee?

No. Buy the hardware once, own it forever. Optional cloud add-ons like off-site video storage are priced separately and clearly — never bundled.

How do I reset a device to factory settings?

Hold the reset button on the device for eight seconds until the indicator flashes twice. You can then re-add it from the Devices tab in the app.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship to the US, Canada, the UK, and most of the EU. More regions are coming — sign up to be notified when yours opens.

What's covered under the warranty?

All hardware ships with a two-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The five-year firmware promise covers security patches on top of that.

Ready to get started?

Setup takes about twenty minutes. No drilling, no electrician, no waiting on hold.
