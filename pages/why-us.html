Our position

Built for the day
after the demo.

Showrooms reward features that impress in five minutes. Homes reward systems that are still invisible, fast, and trustworthy three years later. We optimize for the second one.

01

Local-first control

Your core automations — lights, locks, climate — run on a hub inside your home. If your internet drops, your switches still work. The cloud is for convenience, never a dependency.

02

No subscription lock-in

Buy the hardware, own the hardware, forever. Optional cloud features — remote video storage, off-site backups — are priced separately and clearly, never bundled to force a monthly fee.

03

Five-year firmware promise

Every device that ships under the Stackly Smart Home name gets security patches for at least five years from your purchase date — published on a public schedule, not a "best effort" promise.

04

Plain-language privacy

Our privacy policy is two pages. It says what data leaves your home, where it goes, and how to turn it off — because most policies are written so nobody finishes reading them.
